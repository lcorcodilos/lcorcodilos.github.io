Physics Outreach with Virtual Reality

Spring 2018, 2019

Using personal hardware (PC + HTC Vive), provided a virtual reality exhibit at the annual JHU Physics Fair which is free and open to the Baltimore community to enjoy. In 2018, the experience gave visitors a tour of the ATLAS detector at the LHC via ATLASrift. In 2019, the experience allowed visitors to step "inside" the Belle II detector at the SuperKEKB accelerator to watch slow motion electron-positron collisions and the particle showers that result via Belle II VR.
